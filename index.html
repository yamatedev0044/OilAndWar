About Oil And War

Oil And War — the unflinching platform dissecting the century-long struggle for the world's most coveted resource. From the deserts of the Middle East to the jungles of Venezuela, from World War campaigns to modern resource grabs, we reveal how oil isn't just fuel—it's the invisible hand steering wars, alliances, and power.

Explore explosive investigations, historical deep dives, expert analysis, and the uncomfortable truths governments and corporations don't want you to see. Because the next war might already be pumping beneath the surface. Join the fight for transparency—no more blood for oil.

Join the flight towards a future where crypto soar together, powered by the strength of the oil.

how to buy

1. Download Metamask Wallet

Download Metamask Wallet and set up your ETH wallet.

2. Get ETH

If you don't have ETH yet, you can purchase from any CEX and send it to your ETH wallet.

3. Go To ETH DEX

After you get your ETH in your wallet, you can head to an ETH-based DEX (like the Dextools) to swap tokens.

4. Buy $OIL

Click and select $OIL token or paste the Token Address Contract into the ETH DEX and swap ETH for $OIL.
